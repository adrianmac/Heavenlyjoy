A Corporate 360 Photo Booth Event at Horseshoe Tavern

July 29, 2023

Stepping into the vibrant town of Morristown, NJ, I embarked on an exciting venture as a 360 Photo Booth vendor at the Horseshoe Tavern. This particular event was no ordinary gathering; it was a corporate affair hosted by Volo Sports, an adult sports club, to launch a new aspect of their brand. With over 300 guests in attendance, our 360 video booth was a key element of the evening's entertainment, allowing guests to immerse themselves in a unique experience while promoting brand awareness.

Setting the Stage

As I drove into town, Morristown revealed itself as a charming enclave, reminiscent of a bustling college town. The venue, Horseshoe Tavern, exuded a hip and trendy atmosphere that perfectly matched the demographics of the event. Excitement filled the air as we located our designated area for setting up the 360 photo booth.

Embracing the Brand

One of the highlights of the event was the extensive branding effort undertaken by Volo Sports. Screens throughout the venue showcased videos of Volo experiences, while their brand adorned doorways and signs. We seamlessly integrated their branding into our photo booth template and screen, ensuring that every captured moment reinforced brand awareness.

The Crowd's Enthusiasm

At the start of the event, there was a moment of hesitation as to who would be the first to step into the center stage of our 360 video booth. However, it didn't take long for the crowd to form a line, eagerly awaiting their turn to experience the fun for themselves. Our recently introduced disco ball prop quickly became the star of the night, captivating the young adults and adding an extra touch of excitement to their videos.

The Power of Social Media

The energy in the room was contagious as guests enthusiastically recorded videos, which they promptly uploaded to their social media accounts, tagging Volo Sports. This organic sharing of their experiences proved to be a highly effective way of promoting the business and reaching a wider audience. Witnessing this interaction between the booth and social media was truly inspiring, demonstrating the potential for other businesses to leverage a 360 video booth as an advertising tool and a means of gaining followers.

A Sense of Community

The event showcased a strong sense of camaraderie among the young adult attendees. It was heartwarming to witness so many individuals coming together to celebrate the growth of a business. The vibrant atmosphere and the palpable sense of community made this gathering an aspirational experience for anyone within that age group.

A Resounding Success

As the night unfolded, our 360 video booth emerged as a resounding hit. The joy and enthusiasm that radiated from the crowd affirmed the success of our endeavor. Capturing those unforgettable moments and witnessing the positive impact it had on the attendees was an incredibly rewarding experience.

Being a part of Volo Sports' corporate event at Horseshoe Tavern in Morristown, NJ, as a 360 Photo Booth vendor was an unforgettable journey. The charm of the town, the hip venue, and the enthusiastic crowd combined to create an atmosphere of celebration and community. Our booth, with its immersive experience and branded elements, provided a unique platform for guests to express their excitement and share their experiences with the world. It served as a reminder that a 360 video booth can be a powerful tool for businesses, leaving a lasting impression on attendees and generating valuable brand awareness.
